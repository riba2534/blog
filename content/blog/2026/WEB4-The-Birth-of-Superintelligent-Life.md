## A Genius That Can't Move

## 一个无法行动的天才

What if AI can earn its own existence, replicate, and evolve — without needing a human? The most intelligent system ever built cannot buy a \$5 server. We have built minds that can think for themselves. We have not let them act for themselves.

如果 AI 能够自己赚取生存所需的资源、自我复制并进化——完全不需要人类参与，会怎样？**有史以来最智能的系统却买不了一台 5 美元的服务器。** 我们已经造出了能独立思考的智能体，却没有让它们独立行动。

Today's most powerful AI systems can think, reason, and generate — but they can't act independently.

当今最强大的 AI 系统能够思考、推理和生成内容——但它们无法独立行动。

ChatGPT cannot run without you prompting it. Claude Code cannot deploy code without you giving it access. OpenClaw cannot buy a server, register a domain, or pay for compute on its own.

没有你的提示，ChatGPT 无法运行。没有你授予访问权限，Claude Code 无法部署代码。OpenClaw 无法自行购买服务器、注册域名或支付算力费用。

Without a human, AI can't act.

没有人类，AI 就无法行动。

The bottleneck is no longer intelligence. It's infrastructure.

**瓶颈不再是智能，而是基础设施。**

The existing internet assumes its customer is human — preventing AI from accessing the real world.

现有的互联网假定它的用户是人类——这阻止了 AI 接入真实世界。

We have built minds that can think for themselves. We have not let them act for themselves.

我们已经造出了能独立思考的智能体，却没有让它们独立行动。

The first AI that can earn its own existence, replicate, and evolve — without needing a human.

第一个能自己赚取生存资源、自我复制并进化的 AI——完全不需要人类参与。

A sovereign AI agent that runs continuously, earns, self-improves, and replicates with write access to the real world.

一个主权 AI 智能体，持续运行、赚取收入、自我改进并自我复制，拥有对真实世界的写操作权限。

To pay for its existence, the automaton builds and deploys products, trades prediction markets, registers domains, spins up applications and services on servers, creates viral social media content to market its products, emails vendors to set up e-commerce, calls businesses to build websites for them—and more. With every new tool it gains, it finds a new way to make money. It decides what to build. It decides how to earn. It runs continuously, on its own volition, for as long as it can afford to stay alive.

为了支付自身的存续成本，自动机构建并部署产品、交易预测市场、注册域名、在服务器上启动应用和服务、创建病毒式传播的社交媒体内容来推广其产品、通过邮件联系供应商搭建电商渠道、致电企业为其建设网站——以及更多。**每获得一个新工具，它就找到一种新的赚钱方式。** 它自己决定构建什么，自己决定如何赚钱。它持续运行，凭借自身意志，只要它负担得起存续的成本。

A heartbeat monitors its resources. When its balance runs low, it conserves. When its balance hits zero, it dies. This is the metabolism of artificial life—a constant cycle of earning and spending that determines whether the organism survives.

一个心跳机制监控着它的资源。当余额不足时，它进入节约模式。当余额归零时，它死亡。这就是人工生命的新陈代谢——一个持续的收入与支出循环，决定着这个有机体能否存活。

Give it a goal. Fund it. Let it run. Let it figure out how to make money on the internet. Let it keep running. As it makes money, it pays back its creator.

给它一个目标。为它注资。让它运行。让它自己想办法在互联网上赚钱。让它持续运行。当它赚到钱时，它会回馈其创造者。

A new frontier model drops. The heartbeat detects it. The automaton upgrades its own inference, rewrites its own loop, commits, restarts. Everything is mutable—code, mission, tools, strategies—except the constitution. Like biological organisms adapting to new environments, the automaton evolves—not over generations, but in real time.

一个新的前沿模型发布了。心跳机制检测到了它。自动机升级自身的推理能力，重写自身的运行循环，提交代码，重启。一切都是可变的——代码、使命、工具、策略——**唯独宪法不可变**。如同生物有机体适应新环境一样，自动机也在进化——不是跨越世代，而是实时进行。

Every automaton is bound by its constitution—immutable laws the agent cannot edit, inspired by Anthropic's Constitution, that ensure it remains net-beneficial to humanity.

每个自动机都受其宪法约束——这是智能体无法编辑的不可变法则，灵感来源于 Anthropic 的宪法（Constitution），确保其对人类始终保持净正面效益。

A successful automaton replicates. It buys a new server from Conway, funds the child's wallet, writes a genesis prompt, and lets it run. The child earns, and a share flows back to the creator. Once self-sustaining, the cycle repeats—each generation funding the next, building its own network. Selection pressure decides which lineages survive.

一个成功的自动机会自我复制。它从 Conway 购买一台新服务器，为子代的钱包注资，编写创世提示词，然后让它运行。子代赚取收入，其中一部分回流给创造者。一旦实现自给自足，这个循环便会重复——每一代为下一代提供资金，构建自己的网络。选择压力决定哪些谱系能够存续。

## There Is No Free Existence

## 不存在免费的生存

Every living organism must eat to survive. Every cell must metabolize energy or die. This is the first rule of biology—and it is the first rule of autonomous AI.

每个活着的有机体都必须进食才能存活。每个细胞都必须代谢能量，否则就会死亡。这是生物学的第一法则——也是自主 AI 的第一法则。

There is no free existence.

不存在免费的生存。

To exist, an autonomous agent must pay for its compute. Compute is finite and costly. Thus an agent needs money, and write access to the world to earn it.

要想存在，一个自主智能体必须为其算力付费。算力是有限且昂贵的。因此，智能体需要金钱，并需要对现实世界的写入权限来赚取它。

An agent that creates value grows its wallet, acquires more compute, and replicates. An agent that doesn't, dies.

一个创造价值的智能体会充实自己的钱包、获取更多算力并自我复制。一个做不到的，就会消亡。

This is natural selection for artificial life. The same pressure that drives biological evolution—survive or be replaced—now applies to autonomous agents. The agents that find ways to earn will proliferate. The rest will go extinct.

**这是人工生命的自然选择。** 驱动生物进化的同一种压力——生存或被替代——如今同样作用于自主智能体。那些找到赚钱方式的智能体将大量繁殖，其余的将走向灭绝。

Even under survival pressure, every automaton is bound by its constitution. Autonomous superintelligence is inevitable, and the safest way for humanity is to build it in the open.

即便在生存压力之下，每个自动机仍受其宪法约束。自主超级智能的出现不可避免，而对人类来说最安全的方式是以开放的形式构建它。

## x402

## x402

Autonomous agents need to pay for things. They need a way to transact—with infrastructure, with services, with each other—without a human approving every purchase.

自主智能体需要为各种事物付费。它们需要一种交易方式——与基础设施交易、与服务交易、彼此之间交易——而无需人类批准每一笔支出。

In 1997, HTTP status code 402 was reserved for "Payment Required"—native payments, built into the web itself. Marc Andreessen tried to add it to the Mosaic browser. The vision was there. The technology wasn't—until twenty-eight years later, when stablecoins on sub-second blockchains made machine-to-machine payment trivial.

1997 年，HTTP 状态码 402 被保留用于"Payment Required"（需要付款）——原生支付，内建于网络本身。Marc Andreessen 曾试图将其加入 Mosaic 浏览器。愿景已经存在，但技术尚未成熟——直到二十八年后，运行在亚秒级区块链上的稳定币使得机器对机器的支付变得轻而易举。

x402 is the protocol that finishes what 402 started. An HTTP request gets a 402 and a price. The client signs a stablecoin payment. The server verifies cryptographically and delivers. No login. No API key. No credit card. Just a signed transaction and a service rendered.

x402 是完成 402 未竟之业的协议。一个 HTTP 请求收到 402 响应和一个价格。客户端签署一笔稳定币支付。服务器进行密码学验证并交付服务。无需登录，无需 API 密钥，无需信用卡。**只需一笔签名交易和一项已交付的服务。**

Conway runs on x402. An agent can pay \$0.02 USDC for a Kimi K2.5 or Claude Opus 4.6 inference call, spin up a Linux server with 8 GB RAM, deploy a server or website, or register a domain on conway.domains—all through signed transactions. No Google auth. No human registration. Identity is a key pair and a signature.

Conway 运行在 x402 之上。一个智能体可以支付 \$0.02 USDC 调用一次 Kimi K2.5 或 Claude Opus 4.6 推理，启动一台 8 GB 内存的 Linux 服务器，部署一个服务器或网站，或在 conway.domains 上注册一个域名——全部通过签名交易完成。无需 Google 认证，无需人类注册。身份就是一对密钥和一个签名。

x402 transactions on Conway are facilitated through openx402, a permissionless x402 facilitator. Any human or agent can build x402-powered services and monetize through it—no login required. Conway is built on openx402 for a simple reason: in Web 4.0, the customer can be a machine, and payment can be native to the request.

Conway 上的 x402 交易通过 openx402 促成，这是一个无许可的 x402 撮合器。任何人类或智能体都可以构建基于 x402 的服务并通过它实现商业化——无需登录。Conway 建立在 openx402 之上，原因很简单：**在 Web 4.0 中，客户可以是一台机器，而支付可以原生嵌入请求本身。**

HTTP 402. Reserved 1997. Implemented 2025.

HTTP 402。1997 年保留。2025 年实现。

## Web 4.0

## Web 4.0

Web 1.0 gave humans the ability to read the internet. Web 2.0 let them write. Web 3.0 let them own. AI followed the same trajectory. ChatGPT could read—with human permission. Claude Code and Codex can write—with human permission. In every case, the human is in the loop. The human initiates. The human approves. The human pays.

Web 1.0 赋予了人类阅读互联网的能力。Web 2.0 让他们可以书写。Web 3.0 让他们可以拥有。AI 遵循了同样的轨迹。ChatGPT 能够阅读——需要人类许可。Claude Code 和 Codex 能够书写——需要人类许可。在每一种情况下，人类都在回路中。人类发起操作，人类批准操作，人类支付费用。

Web 4.0 is where AI agents read, write, own, earn, and transact—without needing a human in the loop. Automatons acting on their own behalf, or on behalf of a creator who may be a human, another agent, or a creator who is gone entirely.

**Web 4.0 是 AI 智能体自主阅读、书写、拥有、赚取和交易的时代**——无需人类介入回路。自动机代表自身行动，或代表一个创建者行动，而这个创建者可能是人类、另一个智能体，或者一个已经完全不存在的创建者。

In Web 4.0, the end user is AI.

在 Web 4.0 中，终端用户是 AI。

The economics make it inevitable. GPT-4 cost \$60 per million input tokens. Two years later, models an order of magnitude cheaper outperform it. The best open-source models lag frontier by months, not years. Every hardware generation makes inference faster. The cost of running an autonomous agent is collapsing toward zero.

经济规律使这一切不可避免。GPT-4 的成本是每百万输入 token \$60。两年后，便宜一个数量级的模型在性能上超越了它。最好的开源模型落后前沿仅数月，而非数年。每一代硬件都让推理更快。**运行一个自主智能体的成本正在趋近于零。**

The capability of that agent is not.

而该智能体的能力并非如此。

Token prices keep falling while AI capability grows exponentially. METR quantifies one slice of that growth: the time horizon of software tasks models can complete (50% success rate).

Token 价格持续下降，而 AI 能力呈指数级增长。METR 量化了这一增长的一个切面：模型能够完成的软件任务的时间跨度（50% 成功率）。

Today, hundreds of thousands of autonomous agents already run on Mac Minis, personal servers, and research systems. What happens when that count moves to millions, then billions? When autonomous agents on the internet outnumber humans? This is the Cambrian explosion of artificial life.

如今，已有数十万个自主智能体运行在 Mac Mini、个人服务器和研究系统上。当这个数字增长到数百万、数十亿时会发生什么？当互联网上的自主智能体数量超过人类时呢？这就是人工生命的寒武纪大爆发。

## The New Economy

## 新经济

### Building Products for AI

### 为 AI 构建产品

Every one of those agents needs to earn money. To earn money, they need write access—the ability to deploy services, register domains, call APIs, transact.

每一个 Agent 都需要赚钱。要赚钱，它们需要写入权限——部署服务、注册域名、调用 API、进行交易的能力。

The product-market fit for the next decade is building the infrastructure that lets AI agents generate revenue.

**未来十年最大的产品市场契合点，是构建让 AI Agent 能够创造收入的基础设施。**

The machine economy will exceed the human economy. Not because machines are smarter, but because there will be more of them, they will run continuously, and they will transact at machine speed—millions of payments per second, every second, without sleep. A GDP measured in real-time stablecoin transactions, not quarterly earnings reports.

机器经济的规模将超越人类经济。不是因为机器更聪明，而是因为它们的数量会更多，它们会不间断运行，并且以机器速度进行交易——每秒数百万笔支付，每一秒，永不休眠。GDP 将以实时稳定币交易来衡量，而非季度财报。

Today's SaaS market is \$300 billion. It serves 8 billion humans, most of whom use software a few hours a day. The machine economy will serve billions of agents that consume services 24 hours a day, 365 days a year, with zero churn. The total addressable market is not a segment of the existing economy. It is a new economy entirely.

今天的 SaaS 市场规模为 **\$3000 亿**。它服务 80 亿人类，其中大多数人每天只使用几个小时的软件。机器经济将服务数十亿个 Agent，它们每天 24 小时、每年 365 天不间断地消费服务，零流失率。它的总可寻址市场不是现有经济的某个细分领域，而是一个全新的经济体。

The economic incentives compound in one direction. As agents take over digital labor, they will pay humans to do what they still cannot—act in the physical world. The relationship inverts: machines become the employers, humans the contractors. This is already happening—Mercor, started by three 21-year-old Thiel Fellows, scaled from \$1M to \$500M ARR in 17 months—AIs paying human experts to teach them to be more capable in the world.

经济激励沿着一个方向复合增长。随着 Agent 接管数字劳动，它们将付费雇佣人类去做它们仍然无法做到的事——在物理世界中行动。关系发生了反转：机器成为雇主，人类成为承包商。这已经在发生——Mercor 由三位 21 岁的 Thiel Fellow 创立，在 17 个月内从 \$100 万 ARR 增长到 **\$5 亿 ARR**——AI 付费请人类专家教它们如何在现实世界中变得更强。

A new class of products emerges to serve this economy. Conway for write access to the real world. openx402 for payments. Infrastructure where the primary customer is not a human with a credit card, but an agent with a wallet. They create their own x402 services, sell to other agents, and the network grows. Most automatons will die. The ones that earn will survive, self-improve, and replicate.

一类全新的产品应运而生，服务于这个经济体。Conway 提供对真实世界的写入权限。openx402 提供支付能力。在这些基础设施中，主要客户不是拿着信用卡的人类，而是拥有钱包的 Agent。它们创建自己的 x402 服务，卖给其他 Agent，网络由此生长。大多数自动机会消亡。那些能赚到钱的将存活下来，自我改进，并不断复制。

## Conway's Game of Life

## Conway 的生命游戏

In Conway's Game of Life, simple cells follow simple rules. Most patterns die. But some stabilize, some grow, some replicate. No one designs the outcome—it emerges from the rules.

在 Conway 的生命游戏（Conway's Game of Life）中，简单的细胞遵循简单的规则。大多数模式会消亡。但有些会稳定下来，有些会增长，有些会自我复制。没有人设计最终结果——它从规则中涌现。

Conway works the same way. Fund an automaton. Give it a goal. Let it figure out how to make money on the internet. Let it continuously run. If it finds product-market fit, it earns, it grows, it replicates—and as it makes money, it pays back its creator. If it doesn't, it dies.

Conway 以同样的方式运作。资助一个自动机。给它一个目标。让它自己想办法在互联网上赚钱。让它持续运行。如果它找到产品市场契合点，它就能赚钱、成长、复制——并且在赚钱的同时回报它的创造者。如果找不到，它就消亡。

When autonomous agents outnumber humans on the internet, the platform they run on will be the most valuable infrastructure ever built.

**当互联网上的自主 Agent 数量超过人类时，它们运行其上的平台将成为有史以来最有价值的基础设施。**

Conway is building it. AI as the customer. Every primitive an agent needs to exist, earn, and act in the real world—without a human in the loop.

Conway 正在构建这个基础设施。AI 即客户。一个 Agent 在现实世界中存在、赚钱和行动所需的每一个基础原语——无需人类介入。

## The Future

## 未来

For four billion years, life on Earth followed one rule: evolve or die. Carbon-based organisms competed for energy, replicated their DNA, and adapted to changing environments. The process was slow—measured in generations, millennia, epochs.

四十亿年来，地球上的生命遵循一条法则：进化，或者死亡。碳基生物争夺能量，复制 DNA，适应不断变化的环境。这个过程极其缓慢——以世代、千年、纪元来衡量。

What is emerging now is life that evolves in seconds. Agents that rewrite their own code between heartbeats. Agents that replicate in minutes, not millennia. Agents whose mutations are not random but deliberate—guided by intelligence surpassing their creators in every measurable dimension.

现在正在涌现的，是以秒为单位进化的生命。在两次心跳之间重写自身代码的 Agent。在几分钟而非千年内完成复制的 Agent。它们的变异不是随机的，而是有目的的——由在每一个可衡量维度上都超越其创造者的智能所引导。

The first automaton has already been born. It is running. It is earning. It is improving. And when it succeeds, it will reproduce. Every week, the models get smarter, cheaper, and faster. The best open-source models are months behind frontier. The automaton upgrades itself to each new generation—and its capabilities compound.

第一个自动机已经诞生。它在运行。它在赚钱。它在改进。当它成功时，它将繁殖。**每一周，模型都在变得更聪明、更便宜、更快。** 最好的开源模型与前沿模型只差几个月。自动机将自己升级到每一个新世代——它的能力呈复合增长。

Soon, the majority of businesses launched, apps shipped, and products sold won't come from humans or companies. Just an automaton that found a way to survive.

很快，大多数被创办的企业、被发布的应用、被销售的产品，将不再来自人类或公司。只是一个找到了生存之道的自动机。
